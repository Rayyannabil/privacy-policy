Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Yalla Padel ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you interact with our WhatsApp Business services to book Padel playgrounds.

2. Data Collection

To facilitate bookings and communication, we collect the following types of data via WhatsApp:

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp phone number is your primary identifier used to communicate with our booking system.
  • Booking Details: Date, time, and specific court preferences you request.
  • Transaction Data: Information related to payments processed through Paymob.
  • Conversation History: Chat logs may be processed temporarily to understand your requests and provide customer support.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

We use your data strictly for:

  • Managing Padel court reservations (making, modifying, or canceling bookings).
  • Generating payment links through Paymob to complete transactions securely.
  • Sending booking confirmations, reminders, and court details via WhatsApp.
  • Providing customer support regarding the Yalla Padel services.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data. However, we may share data with necessary service providers:

  • Paymob: Payment information is shared securely with Paymob to process credit card and digital payments. We do not store your card details ourselves.
  • Padel Playgrounds: Your booking details are shared with the specific Padel venue partners to honor your reservation.
  • Meta (Facebook): As this service operates on the WhatsApp Business API, message metadata is processed by Meta platforms according to their own terms.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. For specific step-by-step instructions on how to delete your data, please refer to our dedicated Data Deletion Instructions section below.

User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: October 2023

Overview

At Yalla Padel, we respect your right to privacy. If you wish to permanently delete your personal data from our booking systems and stop using our WhatsApp service, you can do so easily.

How to Request Deletion via WhatsApp

The fastest way to delete your data is through our automated WhatsApp service.

Step 1: Open your chat with Yalla Padel on WhatsApp.

Step 2: Type and send the following exact command:

Delete my data

Step 3: Our system will verify your phone number and process the request. You will receive a confirmation message once your data has been erased from our active databases.

Alternative Method: Email Request

If you are unable to access WhatsApp or have issues with the bot, you may request deletion via email:

  • Send an email to: support@yallapadel.com
  • Subject Line: Data Deletion Request
  • Body: Include the phone number linked to your WhatsApp account.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon a successful deletion request, we will remove the following from our internal servers:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and user profile name.
  • Historical booking data (past and future reservations).
  • Any specific preferences or notes saved in your account.

Important Note on Meta (WhatsApp) Data

While Yalla Padel will delete your data from our own systems, please be aware that Meta (Facebook/WhatsApp) may retain user data on their own servers for their own operational, safety, security, and legal purposes. Deleting your data with Yalla Padel does not automatically delete your account with WhatsApp/Meta. To delete your WhatsApp account entirely, you must use WhatsApp's own "Delete My Account" feature within the app settings.
